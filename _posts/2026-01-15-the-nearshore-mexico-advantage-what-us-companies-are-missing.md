## The Case for Nearshore

The US ServiceNow talent market is structurally undersupplied. Hiring onshore at market rates is expensive, slow, and increasingly competitive. Many companies are looking offshore — but offshore comes with its own risks: timezone gaps, communication friction, and quality inconsistency.

Nearshore Mexico is a different proposition entirely.

## What Nearshore Mexico Actually Offers

### Timezone Alignment
Mexico operates in US timezones (CST/MST/PST depending on location). Your team in Guadalajara or Mexico City is on a call with your Chicago or Denver office without anyone getting up early or staying late.

### Technical Calibre
Mexico's major tech hubs — Guadalajara, Mexico City, Monterrey — produce strong engineering and platform talent. ServiceNow adoption among Mexican enterprises is growing, creating a genuine pool of experienced professionals.

### English Communication
Senior Mexican tech professionals typically have strong business English. This isn't the communication friction you get with truly offshore delivery.

### Cost Advantage
Compared to equivalent US onshore rates, nearshore Mexico delivers **30–40% savings** on contractor day rates without the quality compromise of far-offshore alternatives.
