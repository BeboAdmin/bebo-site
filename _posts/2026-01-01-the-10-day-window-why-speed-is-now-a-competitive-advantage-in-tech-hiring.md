## The Number That Should Change How You Hire

Top ServiceNow and AI candidates are actively off-market within **10 days** of beginning a search.

This reflects the reality of a market where qualified professionals receive multiple approaches simultaneously, where headhunters are constantly working the best candidates, and where an effective candidate has usually signalled interest to 3–5 firms before you've completed your intake process.

## Where Enterprise Hiring Processes Go Wrong

### The Approval Chain
Most companies require multiple approvals before a search can begin, before an interview can be scheduled, and before an offer can be made. Each step adds days. By the time final approval comes through, the market has moved.

### The Multi-Stage Interview
Five stages. Panel presentations. Technical tests that take days to prepare. These processes were designed for a buyer's market. In the ServiceNow and AI talent market, they are a candidate repellent.

### The Committee Decision
Decisions made by committee take longer. When no single person is accountable for a hire, urgency diffuses. The candidate accepts elsewhere while the committee schedules another review.

## What Competitive Hiring Looks Like

The companies that consistently win the best talent share a few behaviours:

1. **Pre-approved hiring criteria** before the search begins
2. **Two-stage maximum** interview process
3. **48-hour decision window** when a shortlisted candidate is presented
4. **Offer authority at hiring manager level** — no waiting for committee sign-off

This doesn't mean lowering your standards. It means respecting that the market is competitive and your process is part of your employer brand.
